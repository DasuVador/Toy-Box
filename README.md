🎮 How to Use
Browse Stations
1.	Open the app
2.	Click any station card to start listening
3.	Use the category tabs to filter by genre
4.	Use the search bar to find specific stations
Save Favorites
1.	Click the 🤍 heart button on any station
2.	Switch to the ❤️ Favorites tab to see saved stations
3.	Click again to remove from favorites
View Your Analytics
1.	Click the 📊 My Analytics tab
2.	See your:
–	🎵 Top genres
–	🌍 Top countries
–	🏆 Most played stations
–	⏱️ Listening by hour & day
–	📈 Total stats
Add Your Own Station
1.	Click ➕ Add Your Radio
2.	Fill in:
–	📻 Station Name
–	🔗 Stream URL (must be direct stream)
–	🏷️ Genre
–	🌍 Country
3.	Click 🚀 Add Station
4.	Your station appears instantly!
